Best Kit (ConvertKit) Alternatives for 2026

Kit (formerly ConvertKit) is excellent for creators — but it's not for everyone. Maybe the pricing doesn't fit your budget. Maybe you need features Kit doesn't have. Maybe you've outgrown it.

Whatever the reason, you're looking for alternatives. This guide covers the best ones in 2026, honestly compared so you can pick the right tool without second-guessing yourself.

Why People Leave Kit

Before we get into alternatives, here's why creators and marketers start looking elsewhere:

  • Pricing — Kit's paid plans start at $25/month for 300 subscribers. That's expensive when you're just starting out.
  • No free plan with email sends — the free plan caps you at 10,000 subscribers but limits features significantly.
  • Limited design options — Kit is deliberately minimal. If you want visually rich emails, you're constrained.
  • Missing features — no native webinars, limited SMS, no built-in landing page A/B testing.
  • Not built for ecommerce — it works, but it's not a natural home for product businesses.

The Best Kit Alternatives in 2026

1. GetResponse — Best All-in-One Alternative

Best for creators and businesses who want email automation, landing pages, and webinars in one platform.

GetResponse has matured into one of the most complete email platforms available. Where Kit focuses on creators with simple sequences, GetResponse gives you:

  • Advanced visual automation builder with conditional logic
  • Built-in webinar hosting (replace Zoom for small sessions)
  • Landing page builder with A/B testing
  • SMS marketing
  • AI email writing assistant
  • Ecommerce integrations (Shopify, WooCommerce)

Pricing:

  • Free plan: up to 500 contacts
  • Starter: ~$15/mo for 1,000 contacts
  • Email Marketing: ~$19/mo
  • Marketing Automation: ~$59/mo

Why it beats Kit: you get more tools for less money, especially at lower subscriber counts. The automation depth is significantly greater.

2. Moosend — Best Budget Alternative

Best for budget-conscious creators and small businesses who want solid automation without the premium price tag.

Moosend is frequently overlooked but seriously underpriced for what it delivers. The automation builder is visual and intuitive, templates are modern, and deliverability is strong.

Key features:

  • Drag-and-drop automation builder

  • Product recommendations (useful for ecommerce)
  • Subscription forms and pop-ups

Pricing:

  • Free trial: 30 days, full access
  • Starter: ~$9/mo for 500 contacts
  • Pro: ~$16/mo for 1,000 contacts

Why it beats Kit: for early-stage creators, the cost savings are substantial. You're getting 80% of what Kit offers at 40–50% of the price.

3. ActiveCampaign — Best for Advanced Automation

Best for established businesses that need the deepest automation and CRM capabilities available.

ActiveCampaign is the gold standard for marketing automation. If Kit feels too simple and you want sophisticated multi-step sequences with CRM integration, deal pipelines, and behavioral triggers — this is your tool.

Key features:

  • 900+ automation triggers and actions
  • Built-in CRM with deal tracking
  • Site tracking and event tracking
  • Lead scoring
  • Predictive sending

Pricing:

  • Starter: ~$15/mo for 1,000 contacts
  • Plus: ~$49/mo
  • Professional: ~$79/mo

Why it beats Kit: for complex funnels and sales pipelines, ActiveCampaign has no equal in this price range. Kit is a newsletter tool; ActiveCampaign is a full marketing stack.

4. Beehiiv — Best for Newsletter Creators

Best for creators building a paid or free newsletter as their primary product.

Beehiiv was built specifically for newsletter creators and it shows. Features like a built-in ad network, referral program, paid subscriptions, and subscriber segmentation make it a natural evolution for creators who've outgrown Kit's simple newsletter setup.

Key features:

  • Built-in referral program
  • Paid newsletter subscriptions via Stripe
  • Ad network access
  • Analytics dashboard
  • Custom domains

Pricing:

  • Free plan: up to 2,500 subscribers
  • Scale: ~$42/mo
  • Max: ~$84/mo

Why it beats Kit: if your business model is a newsletter, Beehiiv's built-in monetization tools are superior to anything Kit offers natively.

5. MailerLite — Best Free Alternative

Best for beginners who want a generous free plan with solid features.

MailerLite's free plan is genuinely good — up to 1,000 subscribers and 12,000 emails per month, with automation, landing pages, and forms included. It's a natural Kit replacement for anyone who finds Kit's pricing steep.

Key features:

  • Visual automation builder
  • Landing page builder
  • Website builder (basic)
  • A/B testing
  • Digital product sales

Pricing:

  • Free: 1,000 subscribers, 12,000 emails/mo
  • Growing Business: ~$9/mo for 1,000 contacts
  • Advanced: ~$19/mo

Why it beats Kit: the free plan is dramatically more generous. For early-stage creators, MailerLite lets you build without paying until you're actually making money.
